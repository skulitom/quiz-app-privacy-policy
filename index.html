Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

Introduction

Greek Letters Quiz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information when you use our services.

Information Collection and Use

Information We Collect

Greek Letters Quiz is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your quiz scores and best scores are stored locally on your device only.
  • Language Preference: Your selected app language (English or Greek) is stored locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Device information beyond what's required for app functionality

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain information to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device and app activity
  • General location information

Google's use of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses data at:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising

Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Or visit www.google.com/settings/ads

Data Storage

All game data (scores, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by other apps

Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to check network connectivity for ad loading

These permissions are only used for displaying ads and do not access your personal data.

Children's Privacy

Greek Letters Quiz is suitable for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Uses AdMob with Google's family-friendly ad settings
  • Contains no social features or chat functionality
  • Contains no external links (except privacy-related links)

Parents: If you believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Since all app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • No data is transmitted over networks (except ad requests to Google)
  • We cannot access your game progress or preferences

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notifying users through an app update (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data on our servers, you have complete control:

  • Data Access: All your data is on your device; you can view it anytime by using the app
  • Data Deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data
  • Data Portability: Your data is only stored locally and is not transferable
  • Ad Preferences: Control ad personalization through your device settings

International Users

Greek Letters Quiz can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't operate servers, data protection regulations like GDPR are less applicable. However, we respect your privacy rights regardless of location.
